homemade shawarma Recipe

create this flavourand satisfying shawarma at home,perfect for lunch or dinner

preparation time

total:Approximatel 1 hour

preparation:15 minutes

marinating:30 minutes

cooking:15 minutes

ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts,sliced thiny
  • 1/2 cup yourgurt
  • 2 table spoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic,minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1teaspoon paprica
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • salt and pepper,to taste
  • pita bread or wraps
  • sliced vegetables:lettuce,tomato,cucumber,red,onion garlic sauce or tahini

instructions

  1. 1. marinate the chicken*in a bowl,mix yourgurt,olive oil,garlic,cumin,paprica,tumeric,coriander,salt,and pepper.Add the chicken slices,coating them well.Cover and marinated for 30 minutes
  2. 2 cook the chicken:heat a skillet over medium heat.cook the marinated chickenfor about 10-12 minutes,until fully cooked and slightly crispy on the edges.
  3. 3 Assemble the shawarma:warm the pita bread in a pan or microwave. place the cooked chicken on the bread, add your choice of sliced vegetables, and drizzle with garlic sauce or tahini.
  4. 4. serve and enjoy: Roll up the pita, wrap it in foil for easy eating,and enjoy your homemade sharwarma!
